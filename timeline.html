Creative Timeline

My creative journey visualized

2018

Journey Begins

Started my creative journey, exploring storytelling across different mediums. Writing very basic stories at first, including the first version of Alvora Chronicles 1.

2020-2025

Multi-Platform Growth

Launched my main YouTube channel focused on film analysis and video essays. Added book reviews and began work on original short stories. Launched gaming channel. Finished writing Alvora and Zehndar short stories. Created this website as a central hub for all creative work.

2026 & Beyond

Future Goals

Focused on developing fantasy book series. Deepening worldbuilding and character development. Working towards book publication. Expanding creative universe. Growing community of storytellers.
