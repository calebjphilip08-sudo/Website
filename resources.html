Book Recommendations

Save the Cat! Writes a Novel

Jessica Brody. Essential guide to story structure, character arcs, and keeping readers engaged. Perfect for both writers and analysts.

Stealing Fire From The Gods

James Bonnet. Deep dive into screenwriting and story structure through mythological lenses. Great for understanding universal storytelling patterns.

The Anatomy of Story

John Truby. Explores 22 universal character archetypes and building meaningful narratives. Brilliant for character development.

Story: Substance, Structure, and The Power of Myth

Robert McKee. The definitive book on storytelling structure. Dense but invaluable for understanding narrative design.
