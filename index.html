About Me

Hi I'm CJ, a writer and content creator from Scotland with a passion for storytelling in all its forms. Whether it's analysing the latest film releases, reviewing fantasy novels, or crafting my own stories, I'm always exploring the narratives that shape our world.

On my main YouTube channel, I create videos about film, books, updates to my own in development book series', writing tips, and video essays on how fiction allows for a deep exploration of real world concerns.

On my gaming channel I create videos on game franchises such as Minecraft, Assassins Creed, Sonic & The Elder Scrolls as well as running a game review series. I'm also working on my own fantasy book series, with the first novel currently in development.

When I'm not creating content, you'll find me reading, watching films, or researching the vast tapestry of human culture & the wonder of the natural world.
