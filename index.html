About the World

Alvora is a world of magic, mystery, and endless possibilities. The world is rich with history, culture, and the potential for adventure. The world is made up of floating continents, each with its own unique ecosystems and civilizations.

The Third Age follows a cast of characters across multiple books, beginning in Year 2033 when a journey sets events in motion that will echo for generations.
