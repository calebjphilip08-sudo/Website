Alvora Second Age: Timeline

An era of expansion, conflict, and the seeds of what was to come

Year: 0

The Second Age Begins

The Second Age of Alvora begins following the War of Light. Darrius Johnathan Aku survives the great battle and helps lead the realm into a new era of rebuilding.

Year: 25

The Founding of Akunda

After decades of rebuilding, Darrius retires from command and establishes the nation of Akunda south of Elandroth, nurturing peace and exploration in the new age.

Year: 56

Darrius Ascends

Darrius Johnathan Aku becomes the first mortal granted ascension, rising as the god of strength and battle alongside the Goddess Valona.

Year: 611

The Dawn of the Duke

David Ralon Seros of Shadowmere leads a mass petition against Lord Vennick's harsh new tax, calling the people to demand relief and justice for the suffering families of the realm.

Year: 611

Shadowmere Crackdown

Lord Vennick's forces declare the protest unlawful and open fire on the petitioners at his estate. The unrest shakes the Second Age and drives David Ralon Seros into hiding.

Year: 612

Outlawed

Official notice is posted declaring David Ralon Seros an outlaw and accusing his followers of sedition. The proclamation marks a turning point in the struggle between the people and the lords.

Year: 1023

The Fall of Telvana

Telvana is attacked by monstrous invaders in a night of fire and slaughter. Sage Celos witnesses the city’s destruction and fights to protect the people before the Third Age is born from its ashes.
