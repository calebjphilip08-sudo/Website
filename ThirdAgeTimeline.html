Alvora Third Age: Timeline

Timeline of major events and book developments during the Third Age

Year: 0

The Third Age Begins

The calendar of Alvora is reset in honour of the loss of the Telvani.

Year: 0

The Fall of Telvana

The great Telvani city is destroyed in a monstrous assault. Sage Celos survives the massacre and begins wandering toward a new life under the watch of the Goddess Valona.

Year: 2

Smith Family Blacksmith Opens

The Smith family blacksmith is opened in Midtown Elandroth. It quickly becomes one of the most successful businesses in the area.

Year: 37

House of Night Defeated?

The House of Night is believed defeated. Their leader Ralos is also believed dead.
